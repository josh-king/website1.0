  • Tokyo Ghoul 13/10/2014 Tags; anime, tokyo ghoul, thoughts

    Welp, Tokyo ghoul ended recently (well actually like a month ago now, but who's counting?). That's pretty much it, the anime was great; the animation, the soundtrack and the voice actors were amazing. Studio Pierrot did a great job with animating all of the battle sequences (how few there were), everything was so fluent and moved brilliantly.
    The only disappointment about this anime is the length. From the start I saw on MAL that it said 24 episodes so obviously I was excited (a well done deadman wonderland and double the amount of episodes?, oh shit) but then halfway through the season, MAL updated the page to show that there was in fact only 12 episodes. I thought this was a mistake mainly because of how spaced out all of the episodes were; everything was being explained at slow pace with a couple of filler episodes, but no.

    So yeah basically fuck you Pierrot for making a great anime end just as soon as something amazing was about to happen. One Eyed Owl.
